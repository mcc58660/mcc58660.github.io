All About Me

Hi there, my name is Madeleine “Maddy” Cho and I am from Suwanee, Georgia, which is conveniently located only an hour and a half from UGA. I am currently a freshman at UGA and I plan on majoring from Terry Business College with a degree in Management Information Systems. I am so excited to be a Bulldog, and coincidentally, I have been a Bulldog for life as through middle school and high school I was also a bulldog. I guess it is just meant to be that I am a bulldog again, and now an official bulldog for life.

Going into college, I really did not know what to expect. I read some college blogs and talked to friends already in college, but by the end of it, I still felt uneasy about leaving home and getting to college. How were classes going to be? How am I going to manage my time? Is college like high school? With those questions in mind, the goal of my website is to share with you and give you a glimpse of a typical day as a UGA freshman. Everyone has a different college experience, and here is mine.
